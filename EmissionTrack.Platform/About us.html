Our Passion is Your Planet

Overview

EmissionTrack is a South African consultancy that helps businesses reduce their carbon footprint.
We specialize in analyzing and managing Scope 3 emissions (indirect emissions from a company's value chain).
Using a cutting-edge SaaS platform, We offer data analytics, insights, and strategies to help companies comply with environmental regulations.

Key Offerings

  • Carbon accounting software
  • Emissions data management, reporting and compliance support.
  • Real-time data insights.
  • Sustainability consulting and strategy development.
  • Emissions reduction project monitoring.
  • Skilled human resources (Sustainability team).
  • Investment and offsetting opportunities to contribute to the reduction of the environmental impact.
  • Employee engagement and basic training programs.
